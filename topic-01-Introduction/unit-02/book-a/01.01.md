# Download Visual Paradigm

- Go to <http://www.visual-paradigm.com>.
- Scroll down the page select the option **Try Visual Paradigm Free**.


![](img/lab1.png)

- On the next page at the bottom, choose **Get Community Edition**.

![](img/lab2.png)

- On the next page you can proceed with the Download or choose **More Options (recommended)**.

![](img/lab3.png)

- Proceed with Download and if you want to download it to your USB drive, choose **InstallFree**.

![](img/lab4.png)
